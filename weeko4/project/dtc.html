How Serious is the Code?

The P0971 diagnostic trouble code is quite serious, because the TCM is not in total
control the hydraulic pressure inside the transmission. This will cause a number of
transmission problems that include slipping in mad or slipper surface, high fuel consumption

Symptoms

Symptoms of a P0971 include:

  • Transmission does not engage all wheel system
  • Vehicle slips in mad
  • Oil Temp blinks
  • Check engine light
  • High fuel consumption

Causes

  • Transmission fluid and filter have become contaminated
  • Wiring has been damaged
  • Connectors are either broken or corroded
  • Malfunction transfer/pressure control solenoid
  • TCM failure

How to Diagnose the Code

The first step to diagnosing a P0971 OBDII trouble code, is to check the level and
condition of the transmission fluid. If it looks clean and there are no metal fragments or
a burnt smell, then the wiring and connectors along the transmission wiring harness
will need to be checked for signs of damage and corrosion. If no shorted wires or
broken connectors are found, then the pressure control solenoid(s) will need to be
checked and valve body.
